# 🪽 Introducing **Icarus Un-follower** — clean up your Umamusume followers on autopilot

Tired of scrolling through hundreds of dead followers? **Icarus Un-follower** finds
everyone who's gone inactive and removes them for you — cleanly, safely, and with
the game closed.

It talks **directly to the game server** (the same proven approach as the Icarus
career bot), so there's no clunky in-game automation. Grab your account once, then
it runs headless.

## ⚡ What it does
- 🔎 **Reads your real follower list** straight from the server — accurate counts,
  not guesses.
- 😴 **Finds the inactive ones** by last login: **12 hours · 1 · 2 · 3 · 7 · 14 ·
  21 days · 1 month**. ("1 month" = a month *or longer*.)
- 🧹 **Removes them in one run** — set **Max = `all`** and it clears everyone
  inactive, or cap it to a number.
- 🕰️ **Human-like pacing** — a randomized delay between each removal (you set the
  min/max), a **Stop** button any time, and automatic back-off if the server gets
  busy.
- 🤝 **Mutual-safe** — never removes someone you follow back (unless you tell it to).
- 🖥️ **Clean dashboard** — live stat cards + activity log, dark and minimal.

## 🚀 How to use it (2 minutes)
1. **Download** and run **Icarus Un-follower**.
2. Open Umamusume to the **home menu** and click **Scan followers** — it grabs your
   account once (tap around in-game so it captures a request). After that, **the
   game can stay closed.**
3. Pick your **inactivity period** + **delay**, set **Max = `all`**, flip **Arm
   removals**, and hit **Remove inactive**. Done. ✅

## 🔒 Safe & private
- Removals go through the **official `friend/un_follower` API** — exactly like
  tapping "Remove Follower" yourself, just automated and paced.
- Your captured auth stays **on your machine** and is never uploaded or shared.
- Everything is **opt-in and reversible up to the click** (dry-run by default;
  you must Arm it).
